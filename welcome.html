Common questions

Things you're probably wondering.

Is it free?
Yes, during beta. No credit card, no trial expiration, no gotcha. If Agntai becomes a paid product, beta agents get a meaningful discount — and plenty of warning.
Will I lose my data?
No. Your data is yours. You can export your leads and transactions to CSV at any time.
Does it replace my current CRM?
It can — but most beta agents run both for a few weeks and decide for themselves. You don't have to commit on day one.
Is my data secure?
Each agent only sees their own leads and clients — enforced at the database level. AI communication goes through a secure backend, so credentials are never exposed in your browser.
How do I give feedback?
Green 💬 button, bottom-right of every screen. One click, one sentence. We read every single one.
